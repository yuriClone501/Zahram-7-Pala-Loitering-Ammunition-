Adds the Zahram-7, a jet-powered Kamikaze Drone.

**Background**

*At the start of the conflict, PALA faced a difficult reality. Their air force, though capable, was stretched thin. Conventional cruise missiles were effective but expensive, and each loss of a strike aircraft meant losing not just a machine but a trained pilot. They needed something in between a weapon that could be sent ahead, wait patiently, and strike only when the moment was right.*

*Development of the Zahram-7 began in the early months of the war. The goal was simple: create a cheap, reusable drone that could loiter over contested areas, identify high-value targets, and engage them without risking crewed assets. Engineers took inspiration from existing cruise missile designs but stripped them down, replacing complex multi-mode seekers with a reliable optical guidance system and extending fuel capacity for longer endurance.*

**Specifications**

- Warhead: 75kg high-explosive

- Range: 1000km (basically can go anywhere)

- Cruise speed: ~600 km/h

- Guidance: Optical seeker (works like an ashm-300)

- Compatibility: **For now, it’s available for all hardpoints with no exceptions. I’m still working on figuring out how to make it usable only on specific ones.**

**Installation**

Move the dll to your BepInEx/plugins folder. The weapon will appear in the loadout menu on compatible aircraft.
